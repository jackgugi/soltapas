Summary

Sol Tapas is a Spanish Tapas Restaurant and Bar, located in the heart of Darlington. Our aim is to provide top quality Tapas, using the best ingredients we can obtain, in stylish surroundings and with friendly efficient service. We hope this website is useful in helping you to get to know us better.

Our restaurant holds 100 seats and can cater for both small parties and large groups. If you would like more information about our restaurant or would like to book a table, feel free to get in touch.

Inspiration

Our tapas menu is inspired by authentic Spanish food, with the option of meat, fish and vegetarian dishes to choose from.

Since the start of our business, 9 years ago, we have taken annual business trips to Spain to bring back only the best Mediterranean cuisine.

We cook only the freshest produce with over 70% sourced from Spain itself. Over the years, our menu has grown to accommodate over 45 authentic items, which are changed regularly to ensure there's always plenty of variety and flavours to tempt your taste buds with.

The enjoyment doesn’t stop at the food however. Our comprehensive wine list and bar promises to have a drink to suit every palate, no matter what the occasion. The heart of the drinks menu being our sophisticated wine list sourced from the best local dealers and shipped in from across the world.
